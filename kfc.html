KFC

KFC is an American fast food restaurant chain headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky that specializes in fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant chain after Mc Donald's with 22,621 lacation globally in 150 countries as of December 2019. The chainis a subsidiary of Yum! Brand, a restaurant company that also owns the Pizza Hut, Taco Bell and WingStreet chain.
