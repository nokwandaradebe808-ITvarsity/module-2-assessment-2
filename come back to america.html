COMING TO AMERICA

Coming to America is a 1988 American romantic comedy film directed by John Landis and based on a story originally created by Eddie Murphy, who also stars in the lead role. The film also co-star Arsenio Hall, James Earl Jones, Shari Headley, and Jon Amos. The film was released in the United States on June 29 ,1988.
